Pluralsight named a Leader in IT training

Learn why IDC MarketScape positioned Pluralsight in the Leaders category and how our IT skill development platform can help your team build in-demand tech skills.

We're honored to be recognized as a Leader in the 2021 IDC MarketScape for IT Training in the United States. Knowing which learning solution will best drive ROI and business outcomes can be overwhelming, and IDC’s MarketScape report provides clarity for leaders wanting to build a culture of skill development at their companies.

As part of the study, IDC MarketScape looked at the factors expected to impact the success of a company’s training efforts in both the short term and the long term. According to the IDC MarketScape, Pluralsight landed in this Leaders category because Pluralsight Skills can help clients customize content and paths; clients appreciate the quality of instructors, presentation and delivery; students appreciate the relevance of its material to their roles and more!

This just scratches the surface of the valuable insights compiled in the 2021 IDC MarketScape report. Download the report excerpt to find out:

• What sets the skill development providers apart

• How technology buyers can make more informed decisions when it comes to IT training

• How Pluralsight’s learning solutions can help individuals and teams stay relevant in an ever-changing industry

• And more
